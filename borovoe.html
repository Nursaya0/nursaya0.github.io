Borovoe tour

The tour to Burabay is an exciting 2-day journey that begins with an early departure from Astana and arrival at the national park. Guests check into a hotel, have breakfast, and embark on a guided tour of scenic locations. On the first day, visitors stroll along Lake Borovoe, hike up Mount Burabay for panoramic views, and visit the famous Zhumbaktas Rock. After a lunch of traditional cuisine, there's time for relaxation or additional exploration.
